About Tutu School

a nationally recognized early childhood dance program

Tutu School is a leading dance and movement program designed specifically for children ages 2–8. With a curriculum rooted in creativity, imagination, and age-appropriate technique, Tutu School classes help young children develop coordination, confidence, and a lifelong love of movement — all in a warm, nurturing environment. Our franchise locations across California, Washington, and Colorado are locally owned and operated by a passionate team dedicated to every child's growth.

About the Owner

locally owned, community focused

Sonya Krawczyk

Franchisee Owner · California, Washington & Colorado

Sonya's connection to Tutu School began the way it does for so many families — through her daughter. After witnessing the joy and growth her child experienced week after week in class, she knew she had found something truly special. With years of experience in business operations, she made the leap to bring that same magic to children across multiple regions.

Today, Sonya oversees ten studio locations across three states under the Twirling Princess, TwilingZ, and Dancing Hero brands. She is committed to maintaining high-quality, well-trained faculty, fostering Tutu School's curriculum of creativity and joy, and engaging meaningfully with the families and communities each studio serves.

Sonya grew up with fond memories of attending ballet and music lessons with her mother — and is proud to help create those same lasting memories for a new generation of children.
