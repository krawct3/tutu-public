Terms & Conditions for Staff SMS Program

Effective April 2026

These Terms & Conditions apply to staff and instructors of Twirling Princess, TwilingZ, and Dancing Hero — Tutu School franchises operated by Sonya Krawczyk — enrolled in the Staff Notifications SMS program.

Program Description

This SMS program is used to send operational reminders and notifications to staff regarding class assignments, substitution requests, scheduling updates, and related internal communications.

Opt-In

Staff enroll in the program by providing their mobile number during onboarding, through internal scheduling or HR systems, or by otherwise agreeing to receive work-related text messages.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on scheduling needs and typically ranges from 1 to 5 messages per week.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from all SMS messages.

Privacy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See the Privacy Policy for additional details.
