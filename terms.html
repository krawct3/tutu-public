Effective April 2026

These Terms and Conditions govern enrollment, attendance, and participation in classes offered by Tutu School franchise locations operated by Sonya Krawczyk, including Twirling Princess (California), TwilingZ (Washington), and Dancing Hero (Colorado). By enrolling your child, you agree to the following terms.

1. Enrollment

Enrollment is confirmed upon receipt of a completed registration form and applicable tuition payment. Spots are limited and are filled on a first-come, first-served basis. Re-enrollment for returning students may be offered during a priority registration period.

2. Tuition & Payment

  • Tuition is due at the time of enrollment or on the first class of each session.
  • Accepted payment methods include credit card, check, and electronic transfer.
  • A late fee may apply to payments received more than 7 days past the due date.
  • Tuition covers the full session and is not prorated for individual missed classes.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancellations made at least 14 days before the session start date are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 14 days of the session start date are subject to a non-refundable registration fee.
  • No refunds are issued after the session has begun, except in cases of medical necessity with documentation.
  • Tutu School reserves the right to cancel or reschedule classes due to low enrollment, instructor absence, or circumstances beyond our control. In such cases, a full refund or credit will be offered.

Regular attendance is encouraged for the best learning experience. Please notify your studio as early as possible if your child will be absent. Makeup classes may be offered at the studio's discretion and are subject to availability. Makeup classes do not roll over between sessions.

5. Health & Safety

  • Tutu School is not responsible for accidents or injuries that occur during normal class activities.
  • In the event of an emergency, studio staff will contact the parent or guardian listed on the registration form.
