Privacy Policy for Staff SMS Communications

Effective April 2026

Twirling Princess, TwirlingZ, and Dancing Hero are Tutu School franchises owned by Sonya Krawczyk. Twirling Princess is the SMS program registrant and provides centralized operational and communications support to all three locations. SMS messaging is used only for internal staff communications such as class assignments, substitution requests, schedule updates, and operational notifications.

Information We Collect

We collect staff contact information such as name, mobile phone number, role, and scheduling information as needed to administer staff communications.

How We Use Information

We use this information solely to send internal operational SMS messages to staff and instructors.

Consent

Staff opt in to receive SMS messages by completing the SMS notifications consent form, which collects their name, mobile number, and an affirmative checkbox indicating they agree to receive work-related text messages. The consent form is publicly viewable at twirlingprincess.com/sms-consent.html. Consent is not a condition of employment and may be revoked at any time by replying STOP to any message or by returning to the form and unchecking the consent box.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on scheduling and operational needs.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from SMS messages. Reply HELP for assistance.

Sharing of Information

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. We do not sell, rent, or lease opt-in lists. This SMS program is not used for marketing; any future customer-marketing SMS programs will be registered separately with their own opt-in. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as messaging providers, is permitted only as necessary to provide the service.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information.
