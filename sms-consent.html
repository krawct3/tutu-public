SMS Notifications — Twirling Princess Staff

Staff opt-in form
SMS is a supplemental convenience, not how we run operations.
  • All operational communications at Twirling Princess — schedules, substitution requests, studio closures, timesheet and certification reminders, and any other work-related information — are delivered via email and Slack. Those are the channels staff are expected to use for work.
  • This SMS program is purely supplemental and exists only as a courtesy for instructors and staff who personally prefer text messages and want a duplicate notification on their phone.
  • Opting in to SMS is entirely optional and is not a condition of employment, hiring, continued employment, scheduling priority, pay, or any service.
  • Declining SMS — or never opting in — has no effect on your employment or access to any information. You will receive every operational notice through email and Slack exactly as before.
  • You may close this page without submitting; your employment status is unchanged.
Twirling Princess runs all staff operations through email and Slack: schedules, substitution requests, closures, and administrative reminders are sent and tracked there. This page is an optional supplemental opt-in form for instructors and staff who would also like the same notifications mirrored to their phone via SMS. SMS is offered solely as a personal convenience for those who prefer texts; it is not a primary or required channel for any operational information, and no staff member needs SMS in order to do their job, receive their schedule, or be informed of anything.

Don't want to opt in? Simply close this page. Email and Slack remain the official channels for every schedule, substitution request, closure notice, and operational update — and you will receive all of it there exactly as before. Your employment, pay, hours, and standing with Twirling Princess are not affected in any way by your choice to opt in or decline. This SMS channel is purely a personal convenience for staff who prefer texts; it is not how the business operates.

What this SMS channel mirrors (for your convenience)

The items below are already delivered to every staff member by email and Slack. Opting in to SMS simply duplicates the same notification to your phone as a convenience. Opting out, or never opting in, means you receive the same information through email and Slack only.

  • Shift confirmations and schedule updates for your assigned studio
  • Substitution requests when a class needs coverage
  • Studio closures and urgent operational alerts
  • Administrative reminders (timesheets, certifications, training)

How to opt out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. Your preference is updated immediately and no further SMS messages will be sent. You will continue to receive every operational notice via email and Slack exactly as before. To get help, reply HELP or email sonya@twirlingprincess.com.

Privacy

Your mobile number and consent record are used only to send supplemental work-related SMS to you. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
