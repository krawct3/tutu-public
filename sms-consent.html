SMS Notifications — Twirling Princess Staff

Staff opt-in form
Twirling Princess provides centralized staff scheduling, substitution, and operational communications for its instructor and staff network. This page is the opt-in form for staff who wish to receive work-related SMS notifications from Twirling Princess. Enrollment is voluntary and is not a condition of employment; staff who do not opt in will continue to receive schedules and operational notices by email.

Don't want to opt in? You may simply close this page. You will continue to receive scheduling and operational updates by email, and your employment will not be affected.

What you'll receive

  • Shift confirmations and schedule updates for your assigned studio
  • Substitution requests when a class needs coverage
  • Studio closures and urgent operational alerts
  • Administrative reminders (timesheets, certifications, training)

How to opt out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. Your preference is updated immediately and no further messages will be sent. To get help, reply HELP or email sonya@twirlingprincess.com.

Privacy

Your mobile number and consent record are used only to send work-related SMS to you. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
