SMS Notifications — Tutu School Staff

Staff opt-in form
Twirling Princess, TwirlingZ, and Dancing Hero are Tutu School franchise locations owned by Sonya Krawczyk. Twirling Princess is the SMS program registrant and provides centralized operational and communications support for all three locations. This page is the staff opt-in form for work-related SMS notifications.

What you'll receive

  • Shift confirmations and schedule updates for your assigned studio
  • Substitution requests when a class needs coverage
  • Studio closures and urgent operational alerts
  • Administrative reminders (timesheets, certifications, training)

How to opt out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. Your preference is updated immediately and no further messages will be sent. To get help, reply HELP or email sonya@tutuschool.com.

Privacy

Your mobile number and consent record are used only to send work-related SMS to you. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
