SMS is optional — a convenience, not how we run operations.

All schedules, sub requests, closures, and reminders go out via email and Slack. Opting in to SMS just mirrors those notices to your phone. It's not required for employment or anything else — skip this page and nothing changes.

Don't want to opt in? Just close this page — your email and Slack stay the same.

What you'll see on SMS

  • Shift confirmations and schedule updates
  • Substitution requests when a class needs coverage
  • Studio closures and urgent alerts
  • Reminders (timesheets, certifications, training)

All of this is already sent via email and Slack. SMS just mirrors it to your phone.

Full disclosure — voluntary consent & how the SMS program works

Twirling Princess conducts all operational communications (schedules, substitution requests, studio closures, timesheet and certification reminders, and every other work-related matter) through email and Slack. Those are the channels of record.

This SMS program is supplemental only. Every SMS message duplicates a notice that has already been sent through email and/or Slack — nothing is delivered exclusively by SMS.

SMS enrollment is entirely optional and is not a condition of employment, hiring, continued employment, scheduling priority, pay, or any service from Twirling Princess. Staff who do not opt in, and staff who later opt out, continue to receive every operational notice via email and Slack with no change to their employment, pay, hours, or scheduling.

Your mobile number and consent record are used only to send these supplemental SMS messages. No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions for full details.

To opt out, reply STOP to any SMS — your preference is updated immediately. For help, reply HELP or email sonya@twirlingprincess.com.
